## About This Event

Hosted in partnership with the **University of Texas Medical Branch (UTMB)**, this educational evening brings together nephrologists, interventional radiologists, and vascular surgeons to advance the standard of dialysis vascular access care.

UTMB has provided comprehensive dialysis care since 1969, when the first patient in Texas began chronic hemodialysis at the institution. This program continues that tradition, gathering expert voices for an evening of education and professional connection.

## UTMB Dialysis Access Program

The UTMB Dialysis Access Program brings together Interventional Nephrology, Interventional Radiology, and Vascular Surgery to provide the full scope of dialysis access care across four campuses: Galveston, League City, Clear Lake, and Angleton-Danbury.
