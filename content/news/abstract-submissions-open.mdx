The Texas Society of Nephrology is pleased to announce that abstract submissions are now open for the 2026 Annual Meeting in Austin.

We welcome original research, case studies, and quality improvement projects across all areas of nephrology and kidney care. Selected abstracts will be presented during the dedicated research session, and outstanding trainee submissions will be recognized.

## What to submit

Abstracts should describe original work not previously presented at a national meeting. We encourage submissions from fellows, residents, and practicing clinicians alike.

## Key dates

Submissions are reviewed on a rolling basis ahead of the meeting. Detailed formatting requirements and the submission portal will be available to members ahead of the deadline.

If you have questions about eligibility or formatting, reach out to the program committee. We're happy to help you put your best work forward.
